World of Dance is a global dance and entertainment brand that began as a dance competition and event company in 2008, later expanding into a televised competition on NBC and other platforms. It encompasses a variety of dance styles and showcases talent from around the world, aiming to unite the diverse dance community. To apply to World of Dance, you'll generally need to submit an application form.
